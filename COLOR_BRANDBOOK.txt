1. RESTRAINT & DIGNITY
   The subject matter requires a subdued, respectful color palette that 
   conveys professionalism and trustworthiness without being clinical or cold.

2. ACCESSIBILITY FIRST
   All colors must meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards minimum (4.5:1 contrast).
   Aim for AAA (7:1) wherever possible for 55+ audience.

3. BRITISH SENSIBILITY
   Colors should reflect traditional British values: understated, reliable,
   and professional. Avoid trendy or overly bright colors.

4. AGE-APPROPRIATE
   High contrast, clear differentiation between colors. No subtle gradients
   or low-contrast combinations that may be difficult for older eyes.
